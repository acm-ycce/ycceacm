Bootcamp on C Programming

Venue : Pioneer Students Council, Duttaterya Nagar, Nagpur
Faculty Incharge:Prof. Supriya Thombre

Speaker : Prof. Supriya Thombre, Faculty, CT, YCCE
Sakshi Kokardekar, Student, CT, YCCE

The Department of Computer Technology, YCCE organised a 3 Days Bootcamp on C Programming on 6th April 2023, 10th April 2023 and 11th April 2023 at Pioneer Students Council, Dattatreya Nagar, Nagpur. The Bootcamp was conducted by Prof Supriya Thombre and Ms. Sakshi Kokardekar on C Programming for the students of 11th Std. The students got to learn about all the fundamentals and basic concepts of C Language. Hands-on-Sessions were also conducted every day in order to help students improve their programming skills. Overall, the Bootcamp was a success and received positive feedback from the students as well as the management of Pioneer Students Council. It was a valuable learning experience for students to understand the concepts of C programming in order to get started with programming.

Coding Olympiad 1.0 (National Level Exam)

On 24 th January 2023, The Coding Cub, YCCE – Codeware and ACM Student Chapter, YCCE has jointly organized a National Level Coding Contest – Coding Olympiad 1.O, with an aim to emphasize the coding skills of the participants and making them aware about the competitive coding expected during campus placement. More than 250 participants across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Uttarakhand and other states pan India took part in the coding contest. The winners were awarded with cash prizes as 5000/- for 1 st winner, 3000/- for 2 nd winner an 1000/- for 3 rd winner. The contest was coordinated under the leadership of faculty coordinators – Prof. Supriya Thombre and Prof. Nikhil Mangrulkar and under the guidance of Dr. Rakhi Wajgi, Head of the Department, Computer Technology, YCCE. Continuous support and encouragement for this contest was provided by our management and Dr. U.P Waghe, Principal YCCE.

SAMPARK 2K23

The Department of Computer Technology organized a “SAMPARK Alumini Conclave” by inviting five distinguished alumini’s namely Mr. Madhao Wagh, Senior MLE/Data Scientist at QuantumBlack, Portland, Oregon, United States, Mr. Atharav Puranik, Quintar,inc, Talks about #ar, #vr, #metaverse, San Francisco Bay Area, Ms. Hashmita Raut, Senior Manager Analytics at FirstCry.com (BrainBees Solutions Pvt. Ltd.), Pune, Maharashtra, India, Ms. Shivani Allewar, Software Developer at Deloitte India (Offices of the US), Hyderabad, India, and Mr. Mayuresh Patle, M Tech at IISc, Bangalore. Maharashtra, India. The conclave's main focus was career counselling by the alumni to their juniors . They acted as mentors by imparting expertise in their specialised professions to students and encouraging them to make wise career selections. They exhorted them to set high standards and go for big aspirations. The interaction with the alumni provided the participants with a wealth of new material to learn and motivated them. The conclave concluded with the conversations with alumni about their difficulties and other aspects of their future trajectories. The Department Head, Dr. Rakhi Wajgi, and Faculty Coordinator, Prof. Supriya Thombre, both contributed crucial and endless support that helped the conclave succeed.

Faculty Coordinator - Prof. Supriya Thombre

SDP on Data Science

Resource Speaker: Er. Sagar Lachure

Description: “Python for Data Science” was a 2-Day Student Development Program (SDP), organized specifically for the students of 3rd semester. Some students from 6th semester who were interested to learn about Data Science, also attended the SDP. The Expert Speaker, ‘Er. UPSURGE 2K22

UPSURGE is a National fest organized by Computer Science and Engineering department YCCE, Nagpur. It is an annual fest where you will witness technical, cultural, social, sports, literature all in one place. Pleasure informing that this year is its first year and with a view to set exceptional benchmarks, the planned events this year are SmackAthon- a hackathon, hands-on workshops, Seminars, a grand pitching event, with a spice of literature and fun of the flash mob. Upsurge as its name suggest A rise in strength, so as to keep alive your desire to rise in! And your desire to win, catch the zeal and compete. We are so excited to have you here!”
