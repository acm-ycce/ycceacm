Versus Coding

The "Versus Coding" competition was organized by the Computer Technology Department as part of Compufest 2k23, and it was conducted online on the HackerRank platform. The event was expertly coordinated by Badal Wanjari and Omkar Kawadghare and was open to all young aspirants. The competition Consisted of three rounds, with each team comprised of four students. In each round, teams were presented with a set of problem statements and were required to compete against each other. The criteria for elimination in the competition were based on both completion time and point accumulation. Those teams that completed the tasks faster and obtained the highest points advanced to the next round. In the final round, the team that completed the challenge the fastest and accrued the most points emerged as the winner of the Versus Coding competition and received a cash prize. The winning team consisted of the following students: Yogendra Bhavsar, Vishal Rathod, Pranay Malviya, and Tushar Kamale.

Faculty Coordinator: Prof. Supriya Thombre, Prof. Kalyani Karule
