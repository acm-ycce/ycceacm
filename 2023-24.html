Versus Coding

The "Versus Coding" competition was organized by the Computer Technology Department as part of Compufest 2k23, and it was conducted online on the HackerRank platform. The event was expertly coordinated by Badal Wanjari and Omkar Kawadghare and was open to all young aspirants. The competition Consisted of three rounds, with each team comprised of four students. In each round, teams were presented with a set of problem statements and were required to compete against each other. The criteria for elimination in the competition were based on both completion time and point accumulation. Those teams that completed the tasks faster and obtained the highest points advanced to the next round. In the final round, the team that completed the challenge the fastest and accrued the most points emerged as the winner of the Versus Coding competition and received a cash prize. The winning team consisted of the following students: Yogendra Bhavsar, Vishal Rathod, Pranay Malviya, and Tushar Kamale.

Faculty Coordinator: Prof. Supriya Thombre, Prof. Kalyani Karule

UI UX BOOTCAMP

The UI UX Bootcamp organized under Compufest 2k23 was conducted offline at the labs of the Computer Technology Department at Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE) in Nagpur. The event was organized by Anup Tarone and Chirayu Sakarkar, and the featured speaker was Ashwini Hingolikar, a UI UX Designer at Medidek in Nagpur. The bootcamp was structured in a modular manner, allowing participants to start with the foundational principles of UI/UX design and gradually progress to more advanced topics. It garnered significant participation from students across various departments who had the opportunity to learn about the insights of UI/UX and engage in hands-on sessions. Additionally, the event also provided discussions on career opportunities in the UI/UX field.

Faculty Coordinator: Prof. Supriya Thombre, Prof. Kalyani Karule

Scripted Secrets

The Scripted Secret event, held on October 12, 2026, las part of Compufest 2k23, organized by the Computer Technology Department, was an exciting competition. Coordinated by Mr. Pranav Pandit and Ms. Vidisha Fulzele, the event attracted a total of 13 teams, with each team composed of four students. The challenge posed to the participants was uncovering a hidden treasure on the college campus. This quest required them to solve a series of intricate codes and follow the provided clues. After an intense and exciting competition, Kirtesh Maskey and his team emerged as the victors, showcasing their impressive problem-solving skills and teamwork. The event was a resounding success, providing an engaging platform for students to apply their technical and analytical abilities in a real-world context.

Faculty Coordinator: Prof.Supriya Thombre, Prof. Kalyani Karule

Git and Github workshop

The Department of Computer Technology successfully conducted a Git and GitHub workshop on October 7th, 2023, featuring Gandhar Patwardhan as the speaker. The workshop's primary objective was to educate students on the fundamental concepts and significance of Git and GitHub in software development. The event catered to a wide range of attendees, from beginners to experienced developers, and provided valuable insights and practical experience. Attendees were exposed to the advantages of using Git and GitHub for managing code, monitoring modifications, and streamlining collaborative efforts. Overall,ythe workshop contributed to enhancing participants' knowledge and skills in version control and collaboration within the realm of software development.

Faculty Coordinator: Prof. Supriya Thombre, Prof. Kalyani Karule
