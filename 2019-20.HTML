Seminar on IOT

The "Seminar On IOT" event organized by ACM in YCCE on 31st January was a highly informative and engaging event that provided participants with insights into the world of the Internet of Things (IoT). Coordinated by Prof. Supriya Thombre, the event featured a seminar that covered a range of topics related to IoT, including its applications, challenges, and opportunities. The seminar was delivered by an expert in the field of IoT, who shared their practical knowledge and experience with the participants. Participants also had the opportunity to ask questions and clarify their doubts. Overall, the "Seminar On IOT" event organized by ACM in YCCE was a valuable learning opportunity for participants who are interested in exploring the potential of IoT

Seminar on System Design

This session helped students to gain knowledge of architecture, interfaces and data for a system to satisfy particualr requirements.

Seminar on Concepts of Data Structures and PLCC

Workshop on Data Analysis

Seminar on Augmented Reality
