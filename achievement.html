Code by the Beach

This is hackathon is a 48our-h event consisting of talks, API demo sessions, socializing sessions and of course a 36-hour hacking session. The initial 6 hours will consist of talks and API demo sessions after which hacking starts. The 36 hours hacking session will be followed by an evaluation of every team’s hack by a panel of judges. The panel will decide the top-3 teams which will be announced at the spot. 

Web Warriors
 Artificial Intelligence and Data Science YCCE

The project was a web app that utilized a supervised machine-learning model with a 65% accuracy rate, developed in under 48 hours. The primary technologies used were Django, HTML, and various Python libraries. Django provided a quick and efficient development framework, while HTML enabled a user-friendly front-end interface. Python libraries were utilized to optimize the machine-learning model. Despite the limited timeframe, the development team leveraged their expertise to create a functional and accurate product.

Smart India Hackathon 2K22

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving. The first four editions SIH2017, SIH2018, SIH2019 and SIH2020 proved to be extremely successful in promoting innovation out-of-the-box thinking in young minds, especially engineering students from across India. 

Team Drushti
 Artificial Intelligence and Data Science YCCE

The Team Drishti from YCCE bagged 1st position in Smart India Hackethon, hosted by Ministry of Education, which was held at Forge Accelerator Tamilnadu, Coimbatore, and The Team won cash prize of Rs. 2,00,000/-. The team members were - Aman Raut, Gagan Loya, Viranchi Dakhare, Valhari Meshram, Atharva Nerkar, and Vedant Birewar. Under the valuable guidance of Dr. Rakhi Wajgi, Head of Department, and the faculties, Prof. Nikhil Magrulkar and Prof. Praful Barekar, the team developed a software for AAC for person with different disabilities, using B²MJ (Beyond Braille Mobility Jacket) approach. With complete enthusiasm, all the team members gave their best and made the department proud

Team Elite Cseian - Computer Science & Engineering Branch

Team Elite Cseian developed a solution for real-time tracking of students' academic progress and learning outcomes across multiple schools. Their solution enabled teachers and administrators to monitor and analyze students' performance using data analytics tools and techniques. By utilizing this solution, teachers could easily identify students who needed extra attention or support and provide them with personalized learning opportunities. The system also enabled parents to track their child's academic progress in real-time, fostering transparency and accountability. Overall, Team Elite Cseian's solution provided an effective and practical means of addressing the challenges faced by educators in monitoring and improving student outcomes, ultimately improving the overall quality of education.

Team iSmart - Computer Technology Branch

Team iSmart developed a solution to address the challenges faced by the government in managing teachers in government schools. The team created a single portal that enabled the government to easily oversee and manage the hiring and posting of teachers. The portal provided clear statistics and data on the current status of teachers, creating a centralized database of information that streamlined decision-making and improved teacher management. By implementing this solution, the government was able to efficiently manage its teaching staff, reducing the likelihood of errors and inconsistencies. Team iSmart's solution provided an effective and practical means of addressing the issues faced by the government in managing its teachers, ultimately improving the education system in government schools.
