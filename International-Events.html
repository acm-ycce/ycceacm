COMPUFEST 2K22

Under the banner of ACM Student Chapter, The Department of Computer Technology, Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering, Nagpur, organised its First-Ever International Level Technical Fest, "Compufest 2k22," from 1st to 3rd September 2022. The inaugural ceremony of the three-day event was held on 1st September 2022, marking the beginning of an exciting journey. Dr. Rakhi Wajgi, Head of the Department, Dr. Manali Kshirsagar, Technical Advisor at MGI, and Dr. A. R. Bhagat Patil, Dean Planning and Development at YCCE were the guests of honour. Compufest2k22 featured a diverse range of technical, non-technical, and cultural events. Over 42 students from abroad and 1112 students pan India participated in the various events organised under Compufest2k22. The events were designed to be both informative and fun, providing an opportunity for participants to explore new technologies and expand their knowledge. From coding challenges to technical quizzes and cultural performances, Compufest2k22 offered something for everyone. The festival was a resounding success, with participants from all over the world showcasing their skills and talent.

Power of Technologies!

An International Event – “Power of Technologies!” of 2:30 hrs was conducted by Sakshi Kokardekar, a 3rd year student of Computer Technology Department of YCCE on Latest Trending Technologies and it’s Applications in various related fields; under Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador Program. During the session, Introduction to Top Trending Technologies were discussed by MLSA Sakshi Kokardekar, followed by the detailed explanation on each topic by Mr. Nikhil Agrawal. Later, MLSA Maung Noor Islam gave insights about Cyber Security. Furthermore, MLSA Khushbu Bijawat explained about Some Exciting Applications of Machine Learning. At the end, a Q&A session was conducted where the experts tried their best to resolve the queries of the audience, and the event concluded with a feedback.

AI on Rise!

An International Event – “AI on Rise!” of 2 hrs was conducted by Sakshi Kokardekar, a 3rd year student of Computer Technology Department of YCCE on AI/ML, Cloud Computing, Microsoft Core Services.During the session,Fundamentals of AI/ML were discussed by MLSA Sakshi Kokardekar. She also demonstrated a live demo of how to Build and Train an Actual Machine Learning Model using a Software-Microsoft Lobe. Later, MLSA Yuganshika Narang gave insights about Cloud Computing, which was further taken into deep by MLSA Makini Brian. Furthermore, MLSA Anika Tasnim explained about Microsoft Core Azure Services.At the end, a Q&A session was conducted where the experts tried their best to resolve the queries of the audience, and the event concluded with a feedback.
